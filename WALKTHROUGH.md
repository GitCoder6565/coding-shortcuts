# 🚀 Antigravity Shortcut Mastery - User Walkthrough

Welcome to **Antigravity Shortcut Mastery**, the ultimate platform for mastering developer shortcuts and achieving zero-gravity productivity. This guide will walk you through the core features and how to maximize your learning.

---

## 🌟 Core Features

### 1. 🔍 Global Command Bar
**Shortcut:** `Ctrl + K`
- Instantly search for any shortcut by **key**, **description**, or **action**.
- Try searching for:
  - "Split screen"
  - "Comment"
  - "Format"
- Use `Arrow Keys` to navigate results and `Enter` to jump to the shortcut.

### 2. 🪟 Multi-Platform Support
**Toggle:** Top navigation bar (Windows / Mac / Linux)
- The entire guide adapts to your OS.
- Switching to **Mac** changes `Ctrl` to `Cmd ⌘` and `Alt` to `Opt ⌥`.
- Switching to **Linux** adapts the `Win` key to `Super`.
- Your preference is saved automatically!

### 3. 🎴 Flashcard "Learning" Mode
**Shortcut:** Press `F` to toggle
- Blurs all shortcut keys to test your memory.
- **Hover** or **Click** on a blurred key to reveal it.
- Perfect for active recall practice sessions.

### 4. 💡 Shortcut of the Day
- A featured shortcut appears at the top of the guide every day.
- Click **"Random"** to discover a new hidden gem instantly.

### 5. ⭐ Favorites System
- Click the **Star (☆)** icon next to any shortcut to save it.
- View all your saved shortcuts in the **Dashboard**.
- Requires a user account (Guest or Registered).

### 6. 🧠 Practical Examples
- Click the **Lightbulb (💡)** icon to see a real-world usage example.
- Learn *why* and *when* to use a shortcut, not just *how*.

## 👤 User Dashboard

### Accessing your Dashboard
1. Click the **User Account** button in the top right.
2. Select **"📊 Dashboard"**.

### Dashboard Features
- **🔥 Streak Tracking**: Tracks consecutive days of learning.
- **📚 Progress Bar**: visualizes your mastery of each Tier (Essential, High-Impact, etc.).
- **🏆 Achievements**: Unlock badges like "Novice Pilot" and "Speed Demon" as you learn.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 (Modern Variables & Flexbox/Grid).
- **Logic**: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+).
- **Storage**: `localStorage` (No backend required).
- **Design**: Glassmorphism, Neon Gradients, Responsive Mobile-First.

## 🚀 Getting Started for Developers
1. Open `shortcuts-guide.html` in your browser.
2. Use the **Platform Toggle** to match your OS.
3. Start with **Tier 1: Essential** and work your way down.
4. Use **Flashcard Mode** to test yourself before moving to the next tier.
